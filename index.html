From Ghana

Greenway Connect Ltd. is a specialized company focused on the trade and transport of Ghanaian timber and other organically processed products. Based in Ghana, the company is dedicated to facilitating the sustainable and responsible export of timber, showcasing a commitment to environmental conservation.

With a focus on quality and eco-friendly practices, the company contributes to the promotion of Ghana's natural resources while adhering to international standards in the trade and transportation industry.

Products & Services

We would like to give you a short introduction about our products and services


Ghanaian timber

Ghana's timber industry thrives on the quality and diversity of its woods, such as mahogany, teak, and sapele. Renowned globally, these timbers contribute significantly to the country's economy. Local artisans, with generations of craftsmanship, create exquisite furniture and carvings, reflecting Ghana's cultural identity. Despite challenges, Ghana prioritizes sustainable forestry to preserve its natural resources. Ghanaian timber is not just an economic asset but also a symbol of the nation's commitment to responsible resource management and cultural heritage preservation.


Organically processed
products

Ghana takes pride in its organically processed products, a testament to a commitment to natural goodness. Fruits, vegetables, and grains are cultivated without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, ensuring a pure and wholesome harvest. These organic gems not only contribute to a healthier lifestyle but also support sustainable farming practices. From farm to table, Ghana's organically processed products stand as a delicious embodiment of nature's finest, embodying a dedication to both well-being and environmental harmony.

core values & principles

We are you're partner when it comes to the transportation of Ghanaian timber and other organically processed products.

Learn more about our core values and principles:

  • Sustainability

    Commitment to sustainable practices in the extraction and processing of timber, ensuring that resources are managed responsibly to protect the environment and maintain biodiversity

  • Ethical Sourcing

    Adherence to ethical sourcing practices, including compliance with local laws and regulations related to timber extraction and processing

  • Environmental Conservation

    Dedication to minimizing the environmental impact of operations by implementing eco-friendly processes and technologies, and supporting reforestation efforts to offset the use of timber resources

  • Quality Assurance

    A commitment to delivering high-quality products to customers, ensuring that the timber and organically processed products meet international standards and specifications

  • Transparency

    Open and transparent communication with customers, partners, and relevant stakeholders regarding sourcing, processing, and transportation practices to build trust and credibility

  • Community Engagement

    Active involvement in the local communities where operations take place, supporting community development initiatives, and contributing to the well-being of residents
